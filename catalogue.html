    Thin-Film Solar Panels


    Solar Tech is at the forefront of solar innovation with our advanced Thin-film 
solar panels. These panels represent a leap forward in photovoltaic technology, 
offering a versatile and lightweight alternative to traditional solar solutions.

    Innovative Technology


    Our Thin-film solar panels utilize a second-generation technology that differs 
from the common crystalline silicon modules. They are made by applying one or 
more layers of photovoltaic material onto a substrate such as glass, plastic, 
or metal.

    Pricing


    The average cost for Thin-film solar panels is $0.50 to $1.20 per watt.
